ThreadCycle – AI-Powered Portal for a Waste-Free Viksit Bharat

Problem Statement

India generates over 7.8 million tons of textile waste annually, making it the third-largest contributor to dry waste. Due to lack of a unified platform, fabric waste from factories, households, and fashion brands ends up in landfills. This not only harms our environment but also leads to loss of economic value and job opportunities.

Our Vision

To transform every fabric scrap into a national asset and make India a global leader in circular textile economy — aligned with the mission of Viksit Bharat 2047.

AI Integration

Our platform uses AI to intelligently match fabric waste providers with the nearest recycling units based on material type, volume, and location. The AI assistant acts as a guide — helping users in real time, answering questions, suggesting recyclers, and tracking environmental impact.
